The Journey of Natima Neily

Embracing Faith Beyond Boundaries

Life is a continuous process of learning and unlearning, and sometimes a life-changing friendship can lead to a newfound faith. Breaking away from misconceptions, such as the idea that Islam is exclusively for Muslims, is essential. Faith transcends boundaries and can be embraced by anyone, as exemplified by Natima Neily, a passionate lawyer and proud convert to Islam. Natima, who was actually named after a Star Trek character by her father, grew up in the Bay Area and pursued a career in family law. Interestingly, it was during her undergraduate studies in Arizona that she met the first hijabi in her life, which played a role in her decision to explore and eventually embrace Islam.

Natima Neily’s Journey of Faith and Self-Discovery

Natima Neily’s exposure to Islam began when she encountered her first hijabi during her undergraduate studies in Arizona. Up until that point, her knowledge of Islam was limited to what she saw in the news. Her upbringing was quite unique; she didn’t grow up with any organized religion. Her mother, who was born in Mexico, didn’t practice Catholicism, and her father identified as a pagan. Despite this, Natima’s journey led her to explore and eventually embrace Islam, demonstrating how faith can transcend boundaries and be adopted by anyone.

Despite not having Catholicism or Christianity in her life growing up, Natima Neily was exposed to culturally spiritual practices rooted in Mexican culture. This led her to believe in the existence of something beyond the physical world. However, it took her many years to move past her childhood understanding of the world. Natima converted to Shia Islam in 2017, five years before this interview, when she was 28. Her journey to discovering Islam more deeply began with her hijabi friend, but it didn’t stop there. She sought further knowledge by reading books and engaging in personal exploration, which ultimately led her to embrace Islam beyond her initial encounter with the hijabi friend.

Natima Neily’s journey towards Islam was a gradual one, taking about three years of looking into the faith before fully embracing it. While she wouldn’t say she was formally studying Islam, she was certainly exploring it. Reflecting on her life, there were numerous subtle moments that guided her towards Islam, culminating in more concrete experiences that led her onto the path of faith.
